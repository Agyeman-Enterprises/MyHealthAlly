## 3. MEDICATION-RELATED SUBMISSIONS

### **A. Refill Requests**

**Process:**
1. Route to clinician or refill queue
2. Verify last visit date
3. Check if appointment required
4. Do NOT promise approval
5. Set status: "Under Review"

**Status Messages to Patient:**
- "Your refill request has been submitted for review"
- "Your care team will review and respond"
- "Processing may take time"

**‚ùå PROHIBITED:**
- Promising approval
- Approving without clinician review
- Changing medication lists

---

### **B. Reported Medication Changes**

**From Hospital, Outside Provider, or Patient Report:**

**Process:**
1. **Upload documents into Solopractice**
   - Discharge notes
   - Provider orders
   - Medication lists

2. **Flag as "External Med Update"**
   - Do NOT update active medication list
   - Do NOT assume changes are active
   - Do NOT promise immediate updates

3. **Route to clinician for review**
   - Clinician reviews source documents
   - Clinician confirms changes
   - Clinician updates medication list

4. **Status to Patient:**
   - "Your medication change has been submitted for clinician review"
   - "Your care team will review the documents and update your records"
   - "Processing may take time"

**‚ùå PROHIBITED:**
- Updating medication lists independently
- Assuming changes are active
- Promising immediate updates
- Making clinical decisions

---

## 4. HOSPITALIZATION / DISCHARGE DOCS

### **Process:**

1. **Upload to Patient Chart**
   - Discharge summary
   - Medication lists
   - Follow-up instructions
   - Lab results (if included)

2. **Flag for Clinician Review**
   - Mark as "Hospitalization Notification"
   - Include discharge date
   - Include facility name

3. **Schedule Follow-Up (if directed)**
   - Check discharge instructions
   - Schedule per instructions
   - Do not schedule without clinician approval

4. **Close Loop Only After Clinician Acknowledgment**
   - Wait for clinician review
   - Wait for clinician action
   - Notify patient of next steps

**Status to Patient:**
- "Your hospitalization notification has been received"
- "Your care team will review and arrange follow-up care"
- "You will be notified of next steps"
