### **Emergency Disclaimer**

**If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately. MyHealth Ally cannot provide emergency medical care.**

**Emergency symptoms include (but are not limited to):**
- Chest pain or pressure
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe allergic reactions
- Signs of stroke (sudden weakness, confusion, speech problems)
- Severe trauma or injury
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe abdominal pain
- Suicidal thoughts or self-harm

**Do not use MyHealth Ally for emergency situations. Call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.**

**MyHealth Ally recognizes that you own your health data. You have the right to:**

1. **Export Your Data**
   - Request a copy of all your data
   - Receive data in a portable format (JSON, CSV, PDF)
   - No fees for data export

2. **Delete Your Data**
   - Request deletion of your account and data
   - Data will be permanently deleted (subject to legal retention requirements)
   - Audit logs may be retained for compliance (anonymized)

3. **Access Your Data**
   - View all data we have about you
   - Request corrections to inaccurate data
   - Understand how your data is used

### **Data Retention**

**We retain your data only as long as necessary:**

- **Messages:** 90 days
- **Vital Signs:** 1 year
- **Medications:** 2 years (regulatory requirement)
- **Audit Logs:** 7 years (HIPAA requirement)

**After retention periods expire, data is automatically deleted.**

### **Data Security**

**Your data is protected by:**
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits
- HIPAA-compliant infrastructure

**We never sell your data. Ever.**
