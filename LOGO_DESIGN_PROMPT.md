# MyHealth Ally Logo Design Prompt

**Brand:** MyHealth Ally  
**Industry:** Healthcare / Patient Engagement Platform  
**Color Palette:** Lavender & Blue Skies  
**Concept:** Two people walking together

---

## üé® **Primary Design Prompt**

### **For AI Image Generators (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion):**

```
Create a modern, professional healthcare logo for "MyHealth Ally" featuring two stylized people walking together side by side. The design should use a soft lavender (#E6E6FA or #B19CD9) and sky blue (#87CEEB or #4A90E2) color palette. The figures should be minimalist, friendly, and represent partnership and support. Include subtle healthcare elements like a heart or medical cross integrated naturally. The style should be clean, modern, and suitable for both digital and print use. Background should be transparent or white. The overall feel should be warm, trustworthy, and approachable.
```

---

## üìù **Detailed Design Brief**

### **Visual Elements:**

1. **Main Subject:**
   - Two stylized human figures walking together
   - Side-by-side, suggesting partnership and support
   - Silhouette or minimalist line art style
   - Friendly, approachable posture
   - One figure slightly supporting/guiding the other (subtle)

2. **Color Scheme:**
   - **Primary Lavender:** #B19CD9, #E6E6FA, or #C8A2C8
   - **Sky Blue:** #87CEEB, #4A90E2, or #5B9BD5
   - **Accent:** White or soft cream for contrast
   - **Optional:** Light gradient from lavender to sky blue

3. **Healthcare Elements (Optional):**
   - Subtle heart shape integrated into the design
   - Medical cross (if appropriate, very subtle)
   - Path or road beneath the figures
   - Sun or light rays in the background (sky theme)

4. **Typography:**
   - Modern, clean sans-serif font
   - "MyHealth" in bold, "Ally" in lighter weight
   - Or: "MHA" monogram option
   - Font suggestions: Inter, Poppins, Montserrat, or custom

### **Style Guidelines:**

- **Style:** Modern, minimalist, professional
- **Mood:** Warm, trustworthy, supportive, approachable
- **Complexity:** Simple enough to work at small sizes (favicon)
- **Scalability:** Must work from 16x16px to large formats
- **Versatility:** Works on light and dark backgrounds
