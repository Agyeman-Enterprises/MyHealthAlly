# Access vs Visits: Understanding Concierge Care Value

## Overview

Concierge care provides value through enhanced access and ongoing support, not just through in-person visits. Understanding this distinction can help you make the most of your care relationship.

## Access Beyond Visits

### Communication Channels

- Direct messaging with care team
- Timely responses to questions
- Coordinated care management
- Ongoing support between visits
- Proactive health management

### Care Coordination

- Help navigating healthcare system
- Coordination across providers
- Support for complex needs
- Resource connection
- Ongoing guidance

## Value of Enhanced Access

### Preventive Focus

- Early intervention opportunities
- Proactive health management
- Regular check-ins and monitoring
- Support for lifestyle changes
- Prevention of complications

### Convenience and Support

- Reduced wait times for concerns
- Quick answers to questions
- Support for decision-making
- Guidance on when to seek care
- Peace of mind

## When Visits Are Needed

### Appropriate Situations

- Comprehensive evaluations
- Physical examinations
- Complex concerns
- Treatment planning
- Follow-up assessments

### Balancing Access and Visits

- Many concerns can be addressed via communication
- Some situations require in-person evaluation
- Care team guides when visits are needed
- Access supports appropriate visit timing
- Integrated approach to care

## Making the Most of Your Care

### Effective Communication

- Use messaging for appropriate concerns
- Provide clear information
- Follow up as recommended
- Engage in preventive care
- Participate actively in your health

### Understanding Value

- Value comes from relationship and access
- Not just number of visits
- Ongoing support and guidance
- Preventive and proactive care
- Coordinated approach

## Important Considerations

- Individual needs and preferences vary
- Balance access with appropriate in-person care
- Communication supports optimal care timing
- Value extends beyond visit frequency

## Important Note

This information is for health navigation and education only. It is not medical advice. Concierge care models vary, and medical services are provided by independent, licensed practices.

## Next Steps

- Understand your care model and access options
- Use communication channels effectively
- Work with your care team on appropriate visit timing
- Engage actively in your health management
