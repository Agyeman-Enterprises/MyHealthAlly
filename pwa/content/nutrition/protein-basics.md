# Protein Basics: Understanding Your Needs

## What Is Protein?

Protein is an essential nutrient that plays many roles in the body, including supporting muscle health, immune function, and various metabolic processes.

## Protein Sources

### Animal Sources

- Lean meats
- Poultry
- Fish and seafood
- Eggs
- Dairy products

### Plant Sources

- Legumes (beans, lentils)
- Nuts and seeds
- Whole grains
- Soy products
- Some vegetables

## Protein Needs

Individual protein needs vary based on:
- Age and life stage
- Activity level
- Health status
- Personal goals

## Timing and Distribution

Some people find it helpful to:
- Distribute protein throughout the day
- Include protein in meals and snacks
- Consider timing around activity
