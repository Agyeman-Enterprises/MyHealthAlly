# 30-Day Nutrition Reset: A Gentle Approach

## Overview

A 30-day nutrition reset is a structured approach to reassessing eating patterns, identifying what works for you, and establishing sustainable habits.

## Core Principles

### Awareness

- Track current eating patterns
- Notice how different foods affect you
- Identify triggers or patterns
- Understand your relationship with food

### Gradual Changes

- Make sustainable adjustments
- Focus on adding beneficial foods
- Reduce processed foods gradually
- Build lasting habits

### Balance

- Include all food groups
- Maintain adequate nutrition
- Avoid extreme restrictions
- Support overall well-being

## What a Reset Might Include

### Whole Foods Emphasis

- Fresh fruits and vegetables
- Whole grains
- Lean proteins
- Healthy fats
- Minimally processed options

### Hydration Focus

- Adequate water intake
- Limiting sugary beverages
- Mindful alcohol consumption
- Supporting overall hydration

### Meal Structure

- Regular meal timing
- Balanced meals and snacks
- Mindful eating practices
- Portion awareness

## Important Considerations

- Individual needs vary significantly
- Work with your care team before making major changes
- Consider any health conditions or medications
- Focus on sustainable patterns, not perfection

## Important Note

This information is for health navigation and education only. It is not medical advice or a treatment plan. Before starting any nutrition reset, discuss it with your care team, especially if you have health conditions or take medications.

## Next Steps

- Discuss your goals with your care team
- Plan gradual, sustainable changes
- Track how changes affect your well-being
- Adjust based on what works for you
