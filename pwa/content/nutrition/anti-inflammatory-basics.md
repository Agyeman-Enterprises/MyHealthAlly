# Anti-Inflammatory Nutrition Basics

## Overview

Anti-inflammatory eating patterns focus on foods that may support the body's natural inflammatory response. This approach emphasizes whole foods and balanced nutrition.

## Key Principles

### Whole Foods Focus

- Fresh fruits and vegetables
- Whole grains
- Lean proteins
- Healthy fats
- Minimally processed foods

### Colorful Variety

Different colored fruits and vegetables provide various nutrients and antioxidants that may support health.

### Balanced Approach

- Regular meal timing
- Adequate hydration
- Portion awareness
- Mindful eating

## Common Anti-Inflammatory Foods

### Fruits and Vegetables

- Berries
- Leafy greens
- Tomatoes
- Citrus fruits
- Cruciferous vegetables

### Healthy Fats

- Olive oil
- Avocado
- Nuts and seeds
- Fatty fish (in moderation)

### Whole Grains

- Oats
- Brown rice
- Quinoa
- Whole wheat
