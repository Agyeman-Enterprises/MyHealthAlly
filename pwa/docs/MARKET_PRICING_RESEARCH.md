## Token Consumption Cost Analysis

### Actual Token Costs

**Anthropic Claude (Your Current Provider):**
- **Input:** ~$3 per 1M tokens
- **Output:** ~$15 per 1M tokens
- **Average:** ~$9 per 1M tokens (mixed usage)

**Estimated Monthly Token Usage (Enterprise Practice):**
- **100 patients** × **10 AI interactions/month** = 1,000 interactions
- **Average:** 2,000 tokens per interaction (1,000 input + 1,000 output)
- **Total:** 2,000,000 tokens/month
- **Cost:** ~$18/month in tokens

**High-Usage Scenario:**
- **500 patients** × **20 AI interactions/month** = 10,000 interactions
- **Total:** 20,000,000 tokens/month
- **Cost:** ~$180/month in tokens

**Very High-Usage Scenario:**
- **1,000 patients** × **30 AI interactions/month** = 30,000 interactions
- **Total:** 60,000,000 tokens/month
- **Cost:** ~$540/month in tokens

**Current:** $200/month for entire enterprise
