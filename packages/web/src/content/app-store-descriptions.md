# App Store Descriptions

## iOS App Store

### Short Description (30 characters)
**MyHealthAlly: Health in one place**

### Subtitle (30 characters)
**Track vitals, stay connected**

### Long Description (4000 characters)

**Your health, in one connected place.**

MyHealthAlly helps you track your vitals, follow your care plan, and stay connected to your care team in a calm, supportive way.

**See your health at a glance**
View your daily wellness score, key vitals, and health trends in one simple dashboard. No overwhelming charts or confusing numbers‚Äîjust clear, easy-to-understand information.

**Track what matters**
Monitor your blood pressure, glucose, heart rate, weight, and sleep patterns over time. See how your health metrics change with clear, visual trends that help you understand what's working.

**Follow your care plan**
Your personalized care plan, broken down into manageable daily steps. Track medications, habits, and goals with gentle reminders that keep you on track.

**Stay connected**
Securely message your care team, ask questions, and share updates whenever you need to. No phone tag, no waiting‚Äîget answers when you need them.

**Get support when it matters**
Receive thoughtful alerts when something needs attention. Your care team is automatically informed, so you're never alone in managing your health.

**Weekly health summaries**
Get a calm, encouraging summary of how you're doing each week. Simple insights that help you understand your progress and celebrate your wins.

**Secure and private**
Your health data is encrypted and protected. We're HIPAA compliant and take your privacy seriously.

**Designed for calm**
MyHealthAlly uses a calm, modern design that reduces anxiety and promotes trust. No harsh colors, no overwhelming information‚Äîjust a supportive companion for your health journey.

**Connect your devices**
Sync data from your smartwatch, fitness tracker, or health devices automatically. Or enter readings manually‚Äîwhatever works best for you.

**For patients, by care teams**
MyHealthAlly is provided through your healthcare provider or clinic. Contact your care team to get started.
