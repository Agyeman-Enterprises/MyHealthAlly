**Body:**

Hi [Patient Name],

Connecting your health devices to MyHealthAlly makes tracking effortless. Your readings sync automatically, so you don't have to remember to enter them manually.

**Supported Devices:**
- Apple Watch (via HealthKit)
- Fitbit trackers
- Garmin devices
- Withings scales and monitors
- And many more!

**How to Connect:**
1. Open the MyHealthAlly app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Connected Devices"
4. Select your device type
5. Follow the connection instructions
6. Grant necessary permissions

**What Syncs:**
- Heart rate
- Steps and activity
- Sleep data
- Weight (if using a smart scale)
- Other metrics your device supports

**Privacy & Security:**
Your device data is encrypted and secure. We only sync the health data you choose to share, and it's protected with the same HIPAA-compliant security as all your health information.

**Don't Have a Device?**
No problem! You can enter all readings manually. The app works great either way.

**Need Help?**
If you have trouble connecting your device, message your care team or check the help section in the app.
